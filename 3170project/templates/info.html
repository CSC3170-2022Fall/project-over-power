Good Hunter

It was always filled with mouthwatering aroma and a great atmosphere. If you can't get your reservations, at least orderingg take-out is still an option.

Wanmin Restaurant

Some say that the goal of this restaurant is to keep everyone on earth well fed, while others believe that it strives to unify all regional cuisine of the world. Either way, with its unique dished, it is surely a place worth visiting.

Shimura's

They say that this is one of the most popular food stores in the shopping district. Though the boss was never able to make it big despite a lifetime of work, he was at last able to gain a little relif of his own here in the shopping district.

Lambad's Tavern

Its owner, Lambad, claims to be a legendary sailor who used to sail far and wide. It is rather difficult to tell how exaggerated that claim is, but juding from the food sold in the tavern, at least the part about cuisines seems credible.
